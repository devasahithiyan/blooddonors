Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood? +

Generally, anyone aged between 18 to 65 in good health can donate blood. Specific criteria may vary.

How often can I donate blood? +

For whole blood donation, you can donate every 56 days. Please consult your healthcare provider for personalized guidance.

Is my information secure? +

Yes, all donor and request data is securely stored and managed through Firebase.
